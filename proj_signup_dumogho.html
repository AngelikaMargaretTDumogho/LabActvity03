Why Climate Action Matters

Rising Temperatures

Global temperatures have increased by about 1.1°C since the pre-industrial era. We must limit warming to 1.5°C to avoid the worst impacts.

Rising Sea Levels

Sea levels are rising at an accelerated rate, threatening coastal communities worldwide and causing more frequent flooding.

Solutions Exist

Renewable energy, sustainable agriculture, and ecosystem restoration are just a few of the many solutions available to combat climate change.
